# Harvest Moon

**Kind:** vendor
**Serves:** cuzzies, station

## Summary
A cross-domain vendor that supplies **both** Cuzzie's and The Station. It lives
in `Entities` precisely because it is not owned by one business — this is the
canonical example of why cross-domain objects never get trapped under a single
domain folder.
