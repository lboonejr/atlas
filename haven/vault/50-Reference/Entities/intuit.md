# Intuit

**Kind:** service (QuickBooks / Workforce — accounting & payroll-adjacent SaaS)
**Serves:** cuzzies

## Summary
Vendor for QuickBooks and Workforce (W2 printing/mailing, multistate payroll support)
for Cuzzie's Dispensary & Delivery LLC. Separate from Gusto (the payroll run system).
Billing tied to a payment method on file; subscription lapses are billing decisions,
never something Samira resolves or pays on Lemar's behalf.
