# Mom's apartment move (July 15) + Grandma relocating from Georgia

Mom (≈65) moves into a new apartment 2026-07-15; movers booked for the big furniture
on the 18th. Plan so far: between the 15th–17th, move the smaller stuff from the
storage unit (same town as the new apartment) over by hand/car, minus the larger
furniture, then let the movers handle the furniture on the 18th.

**Affordability problem:** rent is ~$2,000/mo, more than Mom can carry alone. Lemar
was going to help but Cuzzie's situation cut that off. Grandma (Waynesboro, GA) has
offered to move in with Mom, help pay the bills, and give away her car (to Mom or to
Lemar) — she says she'll bring only her clothes, nothing else.

**Open problem:** getting Grandma from Waynesboro, GA to NJ, and her car up here too,
without breaking the bank or adding stress.

## Options sketched (need Lemar's steer, not urgent)

1. **Grandma drives the car up herself** (~854 mi / ~13–14 hrs, 2-day trip with an
   overnight) — cheapest: just gas (~$150–180) + one hotel night. Solves the "get
   Grandma here" and "get the car here" problems in one trip. Risk: is she comfortable/
   safe driving that far alone at her age/health?
2. **Lemar flies down, drives back with her** — shares the driving, safer, costs a
   one-way flight (~$150–300) + gas + hotel. Costs Lemar 2–3 days away from Cuzzie's/
   Station wind-down work during a busy stretch (court case, insurance deadlines).
3. **Grandma flies, car gets shipped separately** (open auto-transport, ~850 mi ≈
   $700–1,000 via a broker like Montway/uShip) + her flight (~$150–250) — most
   expensive, least physically demanding on her.
4. **Hire a companion driver / one-way U-Haul style personal-car transport service**
   for the car while Grandma flies — similar cost to option 3, less family labor.

**Affordability side:** once Grandma's monthly contribution is known, the $2,000 rent
math can be checked against Mom's income + Grandma's contribution — need that number
from Lemar/Grandma to run it.

Probing questions posted back in the #atlas thread (2026-07-06): how much can Grandma
put toward rent monthly, is she up for the long drive (alone or with Lemar), and does
her arrival need to land before July 15 or can it trail the move by a few days.
