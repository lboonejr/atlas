## What this is
A raw idea: Lemar becoming a licensed notary (or standing up notary services as a small
side offering), on the read that the lift (licensing, bonding, time) is low relative to
the other ventures on the shortlist.

## Open questions (posted as a probe in #decisions)
- Why now — is this a standalone side-income idea, or does it connect to an existing
  venture (e.g. bundled into Cuzzie's/The Station customer-facing services, or the

  Agent (loan-signing work, higher earning ceiling, extra certification/exam)?
- Stakes if it slips 30 days — none identified yet; this reads as opportunistic, not
  urgent.
