# Cuzzy's white-label brand — 100 probing questions (working answers)

Atlas posted 100 probing questions to #cuzzys-brand (2026-06-24 18:15 ET) to help
Lemar flesh out turning Cuzzy's into a cannabis brand that white-labels products
for other dispensaries — covering vision/identity, market positioning, business
model, product, manufacturing, IP/legal, financials, sales, marketing,
regulatory, partnerships, team, and resilience. Full question set posted in
#cuzzys-brand (ts 1782339354.273549).

## Answers so far

**Q1 — What does "Cuzzy's" mean to you beyond the dispensary?** (2026-07-05,
13:14 ET)
Lemar: Cuzzie's is a way of life built on his lifelong friendship with Josh
(CEO) — Josh has been a steady, generous presence through hard times (money,
rides, support for every dream). The idea for Cuzzie's began partly as a way to
honor that by making Josh CEO. That mutual loyalty and lifting-each-other-up is
the heart of the brand he wants to carry forward.

**Q2 — Keep the Cuzzy's name on white-label products, or a separate identity?**
(2026-07-05, 13:14 ET)
Lemar: Genuinely undecided. Locally, Cuzzie's has built some name recognition,
but the business has soured a number of cannabis-industry vendor/cultivator
relationships by owing money — so the name might not land well within the
industry itself, even if it lands fine with customers.

Samira's note in-thread: worth revisiting Q2 once the AP list on the #on-button
canvas is cleared, since outstanding vendor debt is likely a real driver of the
industry hesitation.

98 of 100 questions remain unanswered — no rush, Lemar answers as more comes to
him.
