# Comedy Club — venue scouting shortlist (2026-07-09)

Ran the staged `run:admin-3x` task (project `20260709_comedy-club-venue-scouting`,
staged from #admin 10:13 ET, originating from Lemar's 2026-07-08 20:45 ET ask in
#comedy-club) — searched for open-mic / comedy-suitable venues across Collingswood,
Audubon, Haddon Heights, Camden NJ, and Easton PA.

## Collingswood, NJ
- **Perkins Center for the Arts (The Loft)** — 30 Irvin Ave · arts center/small theater
  · ~50-80 seats · monthly Comedy Open Mic (3rd Friday, hosted by Barry Hollander) +
  music open mics — strong fit
- **The Factory Workers Building** — 13 Fern Ave · renovated warehouse event space ·
  open floor, no published capacity · no live-event track record yet, layout suits a
  stage

## Audubon, NJ
- **The Kove** — 20 W Atlantic Ave · bar/restaurant with banquet hall · Grand Ballroom
  up to 200 · regularly hosts live music/entertainment

## Haddon Heights, NJ
- **Tanner Brewing Company** — 222 W Atlantic Ave · brewery/taproom · intimate ·
  weekly trivia + "Tanner Talent" open mic (1st & 3rd Wed) + live music Fri/Sat —
  strong fit
- **Reunion Hall** — Haddon Township (~2mi away) · large multi-concept brewery/bar/
  event space · 5,000 sqft indoor + 4,000 sqft outdoor, 56 taps · no confirmed comedy
  history but plenty of room

## Camden, NJ
- **Freedom Mortgage Pavilion** — 1 Harbour Blvd · amphitheater + indoor winter
  theater · 7,000-25,000 cap · already books touring comedy acts — too big for an
  open mic but confirms local demand
- **Donkey's Place** — 1223 Haddon Ave · historic neighborhood bar since 1943 ·
  small/intimate · has a "Bar Events" program, no confirmed recurring live
  entertainment — weaker signal

## Easton, PA
- **Pearly Baker's Alehouse** — 11 Centre Square · alehouse/bar · mid-size · weekly
  Monday open mic + trivia/karaoke/live bands — strong fit
- **Tapestry Lounge & Cafe** — downtown Easton · lounge/cafe · small/intimate ·
  weekly Monday open mic (musicians/poets/singers) — strong fit
- **Black & Blue** — 683 Walnut St · bar/restaurant · live music + Thursday karaoke +
  open mic nights — strong fit

## Recommendation
Best first-pitch shortlist (already run open mics, so an owner ask is an easy yes):
Perkins Center (Collingswood), Tanner Brewing (Haddon Heights), Pearly Baker's /
Tapestry Lounge / Black & Blue (Easton).
