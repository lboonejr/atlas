# Free Books Project — Community Literacy Partnership (CCPUL)

Lemar opened a dedicated channel, #free-books-partnership, and posted a full worked-up
partnership brief. Explicitly **parked**: develop for the reopening scenario, not for
immediate execution during wind-down.

## Partner
The Free Books Project, aka Camden County Pop Up Library (CCPUL). Founder/ED: [[tom-martin]].
Camden literacy nonprofit — 250k+ books distributed, 100+ "Book Arks" across Camden
County, weekly pop-up libraries. Model: free books via pop-ups + permanent Book Arks
placed at community sites (soup kitchens, clinics, transit hubs). They take books where
libraries don't go and give them away.

## Objective
Position Cuzzie's as a community education partner in Camden, anchored on drug-policy
history / war-on-drugs / social-justice education. Sits inside the Cuzzie's brand
(community, education, experience) and mirrors NJ's own social-equity rationale for
legalization.

## Structure — two tracks
- **Track A — Public sponsored Book Ark (partnership core).** Cuzzie's funds + decorates
  an ark (brand colors, sponsor plaque); Tom's team places it at one of their existing
  partner sites. Curation leans war-on-drugs history, mass incarceration,
  expungement/reentry, social equity. Cuzzie's = sponsor + steward. Sponsorship
  decouples brand/dollars from Cuzzie's real estate — the ark lives on the partner's
  turf, reaching the population the mission is built for.
- **Track B — In-store 21+ shelf (Cuzzie's-owned amenity).** Responsible-use + product
  education + cannabis-history/justice titles for a verified 21+ audience. Buildable
  independently; nonprofit involvement optional.

## Funding entry points (pick one to lead)
- Sponsor an ark's construction/adoption (construction is their biggest cost — concrete,
  meaningful).
- Fund Spanish-language books (known unmet demand; clean quotable story).
- Staff-steward an existing nearby ark (sweat equity, genuine involvement).

## Compliance gate — HARD, clears before anything goes live
NJ CRC advertising-vs-education line; youth-audience and marketing rules (apply even to
21+ audiences); no health claims; framing must be sponsorship, not an ad channel. Route
through Reggie + counsel. Not a formality.

## Partner dynamics
Must be Tom's call. Lead with community/literacy value, Cuzzie's as supporter — not a
brand takeover. Deal dies the moment it looks like their infrastructure is being used as
advertising.
