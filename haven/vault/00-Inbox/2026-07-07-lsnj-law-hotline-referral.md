# LSNJ Law Hotline — no direct representation, referred to private attorneys

Lemar called the LSNJ (Legal Services of New Jersey) Law Hotline about an unspecified
legal matter. LSNJ's Law Hotline serves eligible low-income applicants for NJ civil
legal matters at no cost — it did not take the case (income-eligibility or
scope-of-service mismatch, not stated which). Their auto-reply pointed to two
self-serve options instead of representation:

1. **NJ Bar Associations by county** — some county bars run their own lower-fee
   referral programs; contact the relevant county bar directly.
2. **Justia Lawyer Referral Service** — justia.com/lawyers, a private-attorney
   directory (fees apply, no guarantee of representation).
