# Cuzzie's Reopening Plan — draft brief for today's Josh meeting

## Why reopen / the case
Cuzzie's built real name recognition in Camden as a budget-friendly, culturally-aligned
dispensary — that local equity is still intact even though retail ops have been paused
since 2026-06-13. The business was built on Lemar and Josh's lifelong friendship (Josh
was made CEO partly to honor that bond), and the "family" ethos Lemar wants customers to
feel is the same thing keeping vendors, Jerzey Grown, and Eddie Osefo willing to keep
talking through the wind-down. This is a temporary-closure story, not a shutdown — the
plan below is about proving there's a credible, sequenced path back to open doors, which
is exactly what investors need to see before they'll commit.

## What has to be resolved first
- **MCA coverage decision** — BizFundsHub can only fund 2 of Cuzzie's 3 MCAs. Lemar
  decided 7/8 to let **Liquidibee** go uncovered, keeping Novus and Elevate current. No
  dollar terms from BizFundsHub yet; the Liquidibee negotiate-or-extend track is still open.
- **Harrison Acquisitions lease/eviction** — trial July 30, arrears now ~$60K (up from
  $42,157.37). Landlord's counsel will consent to a 30-day adjournment only if **$20,000
  is paid immediately** (demand as of 7/9). Lemar asked this morning (7/10) about filing
  directly with the court instead — added as Option D on the open #decisions card, still
  unresolved. 9 days of runway before trial.
- **Vendor debt overhang** — known past-due AP: Weedmaps ($6,583 batch + a separate
  unpriced invoice), Little Leaf Labs ($8,331, collections-threat stage), Bud's Goods
  ($1,512), Cintas ($226), ADT ($1,638), Leafly ($279), Ambotte Mechanical ($432,
  acknowledged), Mule Extracts (amount unconfirmed) — roughly **$19K+ in named vendor
  invoices**. The full On-Button Reopen Checklist total (incl. Parke Bank overdraft, a
  $40K intercompany inventory loan, and a $4K personal loan) is **~$87,056** as of 7/9.
- **Payroll/Gusto stability** — repeated funding-shortfall alerts (Jul 3, 7, 8, 14) tied
  to an overdrawn Parke Bank account; next payroll due Jul 13 for the Jun 28–Jul 11 period.
- **EPLI insurance lapsed** — Lemar's 7/6 call: let it lapse ($4,051.12) for now,
  reinstate at reopening. Progressive Commercial auto also cancelled, $1,107.20 owed.
- **CRC compliance** — agent-affiliation cessation processed for 5 agents; temporary-
  closure status already disclosed to CRC investigator Sauchelli as financially driven.
  One open commitment: the chain-of-custody/secure-transport procedures write-up
  promised to CRC hasn't gone out yet.

## What's already moving in Cuzzie's favor
- **Jerzey Grown** (Jarred Freeman) — positive 7/6 call, offering product and possibly
  staff support to help get back on feet; brand alignment noted; proposal still pending.
- **Eddie Osefo** (Happy Eddie) — positive 7/6 call, wants to support; follow-up email
  with pitch deck drafted; white-label angle still to be fleshed out before raising it.
- **FundCanna** — full restructure application (all 3 MCA agreements, 11mo bank
  statements, payoff docs) sent 7/8 to Sal Castaneda; confirmed received, under review —
  a real shot at consolidating the 3 MCAs into one Operating Capital Line.
- **Highgate Capital** — broker engagement to sell the business exists as a parallel
  option; Lemar declined the retainer 7/7 on cash grounds but left the door open.
- **White-label brand direction** — Cuzzie's name decision made (keep it); several of
  the 100 brand-strategy questions answered, giving investors a forward-looking growth
  story beyond the single Camden storefront.

## Open questions for the Josh meeting today
1. Given the 7/30 Harrison trial and the $20K adjournment demand, what's a realistic
   **target reopen date** — and does it hinge on resolving the lease first?
2. What's **Josh's specific role** in the reopening push — capital (FundCanna/
   BizFundsHub relationships he's already driving) or ops/compliance?
3. **Sequencing** — resolve the MCA/lease/vendor blockers before approaching investors,
   or use investor capital itself to clear them?
4. Cuzzie's is running **reopen and sell tracks in parallel** (Highgate broker
   engagement, the Jason Klein/Cann.Dev Camden buyer process) — do we present investors
   a reopening-only story, or keep the sale option visible as a fallback?
5. How soon after the lease/funding questions land can Cuzzie's realistically
   **restore CRC agent affiliations** and pass compliance review to legally reopen?
