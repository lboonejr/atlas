# Google Cloud Identity — mandatory 2SV enforcement starting Oct 20, 2026

Automated (no-reply) mandatory service announcement from Google Cloud, received
2026-07-23 (~9:08am ET), addressed to Lemar as the Cloud Identity org admin.

## What it says
Starting **October 20, 2026**, users signing in with native Cloud Identity accounts
must use 2-Step Verification (2SV/MFA) to reach the Google Cloud console. Does NOT
apply to users on Single Sign-On, and does NOT affect core Workspace products
(Gmail, Sheets, Slides — those have their own separate 2SV programs).

Google is offering two self-service options in the Cloud console ahead of the
deadline:
- **90-day enforcement extension** (buys more prep time)
- **Opt-out** (Google explicitly advises against this)

## Suggested actions before Oct 20, 2026
1. Review/apply the self-service 2SV controls in the Google Cloud console (only
   the org admin — Lemar — can do this).
2. If any Cuzzie's staff sign in to Cloud Identity (not just Workspace/Gmail),
   let them know 2SV will be required.
3. Decide: enforce now, take the 90-day extension, or (not recommended) opt out.

No dollar amount, no lender/regulator on this thread — plain IT/security
housekeeping. Long lead time (about 3 months out), low urgency today.
