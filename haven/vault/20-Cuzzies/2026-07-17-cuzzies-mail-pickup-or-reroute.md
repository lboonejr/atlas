# Cuzzie's mail — pickup vs. reroute

Since Cuzzie's has been closed, no mail is being received there. Lemar asked
whether there's somewhere to physically go pick mail up, or whether it actually
needs to be rerouted/forwarded to another address.

## What's needed
Research USPS options for the Cuzzie's business address: (a) can mail be held
for pickup at the servicing post office, or (b) does it need a mail-forwarding
request to a different address. Report findings back so Lemar can pick a path —
this is a decision only he can make (which address to reroute to, if any).

## Update 2026-07-17T17:20-04:00 — research answer given
Lemar nudged ("Any answers on this?") in the capture DM. Answered directly with
the two USPS paths:
1. **Pick up in person** — bring photo ID to the post office serving 2764 Mt
   Ephraim Ave, Camden, ask for held mail for "Cuzzie's Dispensary & Delivery,
   LLC."
2. **Reroute** — file a USPS Change of Address (usps.com/manage/forward.htm or
   Form 3575) to redirect business mail elsewhere (home, GreenBooks, a PO box).
   Requires proof of authorization (LLC paperwork/EIN letter); takes a few days
   to take effect.
Open: which path Lemar wants. Nothing actioned by Samira — no USPS account
access.
