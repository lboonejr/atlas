# Cuzzie's mail pickup / reroute since closure

Since Cuzzie's (Camden, 2764 Mt Ephraim Ave) closed, no mail has been received. Lemar
asked in the Samira capture DM whether mail can just be picked up or needs to be
rerouted. Samira answered (2026-07-17): mail is not rerouted automatically — USPS keeps
attempting delivery / holds it at the local post office until one of:

1. **Pick up in person** — bring photo ID to the post office serving 2764 Mt Ephraim Ave
   (Camden), ask for held mail for "Cuzzie's Dispensary & Delivery, LLC."
2. **Reroute** — file a USPS Change of Address for the business at
   usps.com/manage/forward.htm (or in person, Form 3575). Needs proof of authorization
   (LLC paperwork / EIN letter); takes a few days to take effect, and mail already in
   transit before that won't follow.

This needs Lemar's ID/account in person or on the USPS site — Samira cannot do it on
his behalf. Lemar asked (2026-07-18) to log this as a to-do with a `due` so it doesn't
slip.
