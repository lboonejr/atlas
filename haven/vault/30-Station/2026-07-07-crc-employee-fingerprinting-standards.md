# CRC — employee fingerprinting standards (informational)

Mario Bellusci (CRC Investigator) circulated fingerprinting standards, forwarded by
Markony Monteiro to Diana (Station) and Lemar. No action needed — informational only.

- **Owner/Principal/Supervisory employee** → must be fingerprinted, wait for
  clearance before starting work.
- **Non-supervisory employee** → no fingerprints required.
- NJ-CRC waived fingerprinting for "employees"/"volunteers" under Adult-Use (AU)
  regulations as of 2026-05-14. AU-only facility staff get a badge on standard
  onboarding, no fingerprint required.
- If a non-supervisory employee is promoted into management, fingerprinting then
  applies.

Relevant to both The Station (Newark) and Cuzzie's onboarding going forward.
